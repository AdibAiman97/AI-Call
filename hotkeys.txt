Ctrl+Shift+G:

*Initiates a call.
*Ends an active call and navigates to the call summary.
*Admin View: Accesses the appointment tab.

Ctrl+Shift+B:

*Returns to the homepage from the call summary.
*Admin View: Accesses the summary tab.

Ctrl+Shift+R: 
*Toggles between User and Admin roles.

Ctrl+Shift+D:
*Navigates to the FAQ database.

Ctrl+Shift+H: 
*Admin View: Navigates to the Admin homepage.
